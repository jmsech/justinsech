Captain Shmule (I play guitar!)

A welcoming band with a rotating cast. Minimal instrument experience required, all we ask is you be a part of the fun.

One of the great things about college is meeting people with similar interests as you but with completely different backgrounds. My roomate (our drummer) and I started playing together our sophmore year of college, and we soon recruited any and all of our friends who play (or "play") an instrument to come jam with us. Since then, the band has heard 12 different people, either in gigs or just "jamming" in our tiny appartment, and we're always looking for more voices to add to our sound. Now, what is our sound exactly? With as many cast members as we've had its hard to put a finger on it, but we are currently most influeced by blues and psychedellic rock. Keep your ears pealed for the Captain Smule EP coming soon!

In 2018 we held a concert t-shirt fundraiser that raised over $200 dolars for the Baltrimore Youth Arts program.

3203 Brewing Company

My hometown, Asheville, NC, has been coined "Beer City USA" and is one of the fasted growing microbrewing scenes in the nation. At the start of this semester, two friends and I decided to embark on our own craft-brewing experiment. Since August 2020, we have expanded our operation from just 9-bottles per batch to 50-bottles each and have produced 5 batches (250 bottles) of beer and hard cider including a Bavarian Hefeweizen, an Amber Ale, a Citrus IPA, and a Mapple Cinnamon Apple Cider. Over the course of the semester I've learned a ton about the fermentation process, sourcing various ingredients, and a whole lot of sanitization skills.
